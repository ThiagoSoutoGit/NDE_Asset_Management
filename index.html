ATC - Asset Management

An Asset Management Tool created by Skinny Robot

Get Started â†’

Assets

Create assets in a quick and easy way. Set name and description and them track them back, group them, and much more.

Live Update

Assets are updated live and automatically. No need to inform anybody anymore simply track your assets and know their status right away.

Documents Page

All your documents on the same place sorted anf filtered as you like.

File Requests

Create an Asset and require files to be Uploaded, so you can share it and people know what to upload once they complete their jobs.

QR code

Simply Scan a QR code and go direct to the asset of your interest, never loose time anymore to find and distribute documents on the job.

Permissions

Strong permissions system, your files will always be safe. Define quickly Who can to read, update, attach, share or delete your assets.

Map View

Have all your assets on a map and just click on them to go directly to the asset.

GPS Location

Easily locate your assets and track them to their position. Get directions, print maps and much more.

FLAG System

Is a document too important, going to expire or in need of attention? The FLAG system will prioritize them on the asset page and classify them as red, yellow and green.

Responsive and Intuitive Design

Manage your assets from any Device. A intuitive design will guide you throughout the whole application.
